Life after Class 10
The journey of education never stops! Life after Class 10 is a struggle. If you can cross this hurdle, you will definitely get into some good college but your journey goes on.
 
You have to choose one of the following streams in your 11th standard-
1.Science 2.Humanities/Arts 3.Commerce
 
It is upto your interest to choose from them. So be wise.
 
Science
If you chose Science, then you can learn subjects such as Physics,Chemistry,Biology,Maths,etc.
 
You can give competitive examinations such as IIT-JEE,NEET,KVPY,etc.
 
Career options: There are many to choose from. You can become an Engineer, a Doctor, a Data Scientist, etc.
Arts/Humanities
If you chose Arts, then you can learn subjects such as Geography,History,Political Science,Psychology,etc.
 
You can give competitive examinations such as NID Entrance Exam, NIFT Entrance Exam,etc.
 
Career options: There are many to choose from. You can become a Fashion/Interior Designer,Creative Director/Media,Manager/Journalist,Growth Hacker,etc.
Commerce
If you chose Commerce, then you can learn subjects such as Economics,Business Studies,English,Mathematics,etc.
 
You can give competitive examinations such as CA Common Proficiency Test(CPT),St Xavier Entrance Test,Company Secretary Executive Entrance Exam (CSEET).
 
Career options: There are many to choose from. You can become a Certified Management Accountant (CMA),Human Resource Manager, Certified Public Accountant (CPA),Product Manager,Chartered Accountant (CA),etc.
